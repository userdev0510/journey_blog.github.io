Add new awesome features to the WordPress editor with Superb Gutenberg blocks!

== Description ==

Use Superb Gutenberg Blocks to add new awesome features to the WordPress editor in seconds!

Superb Gutenberg Blocks is GDPR compliant, lightweight and responsive.
The plugin is actively developed & new blocks will be added over time, all the block designs are designed to increase your conversion.
	
<strong>About The Author Block</strong> 
<p>Use this block to easily add a section in the end of your articles where you showcase the article author, link to his/hers social media. You can either get the info from their WordPress user profile or write a fresh one.</p>
<ul>
	<li>Import author data from Users or write new author info</li>
	<li>Translate or customize the "About The Author" headline</li>
	<li>Add an author bio</li>
	<li>Add Author instagram link</li>
	<li>Add author Facebook link</li>
	<li>Add author twitter link</li>
</ul>

<strong>Table Of Contents Block</strong>
<p>Improve your search engine optimization (SEO) and make your articles more userfriendly by using our table of contents block. It'll list all article headings with hotlinks so your users an easily skip to a certain part of an article.</p>
<ul>
	<li>List all headlines</li>
	<li>List headlines in hierarchy</li>
	<li>Minimal & easy readable design</li>
	<li>Re-check all headings easily</li>
	<li>Translate/customize TTable Of Contents title</li>
</ul>

<strong>Rating Block</strong>
<p>If you own an affiliate website, review products or have your own products then our rating block will help you easily display your ratings in an elegant way. Using a block to display ratings is the fastest way to display your product ratings.</p>
<ul>
	<li>Add ratings quick & easy to products, pages & posts</li>
	<li>Customize/translate rating headline</li>
	<li>Add unlimited rating bars</li>
	<li>Add headlines to each rating bar</li>
	<li>Use rating bar average as overall rating</li>
	<li>Choose your own rating scale, ie. 1-5 or 1-100</li>
</ul>
